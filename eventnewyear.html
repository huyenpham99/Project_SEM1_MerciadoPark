Welcome the new year with our park

09:19 07/12/2019 - Luong Dinh Dai

At the end of the year, Merciado Amusement Park, again becomes a land of light, attracting tourists from all over the world to celebrate the New Year in the most jubilant way.

Merciado Amusement Park does not limit the number of participants, but if the number of visitors becomes too crowded, the park will be forced to stop selling tickets. Therefore, most people choose to book in advance. In addition, viewers have to go to the park from the morning, although the parade takes place in the afternoon, if you want to keep a good place for yourself and your family

About Us

Our Park

Merciado Amusement Park is a company whose main activity is to provide entertainment services.

In the 90 of the last century, Hanoi did not have a modern entertainment area worthy of the position of the Capital, while the entertainment needs of the people of the Capital and the Northern provinces were very big. Facing this fact, investors came up with the idea to build a modern entertainment complex in West Lake to meet the legitimate needs of people.

Over 5 years of formation and development, with many ups and downs of history, the next generation follows the previous generation; We Merciado officials, workers and employees together built a strong solidarity Merciado today. Here, there are people living, sticking together emotionally, with sincerity and above all, we share the same ideals, the same passion for labor, tireless creation ... All all of these have created a unique cultural beauty called Merciado Culture.
